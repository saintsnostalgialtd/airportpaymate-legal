Privacy Policy

App: AirportPayMate
Company: Saints Nostalgia Ltd
Last updated: 17 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AirportPayMate collects, uses, and protects your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

AirportPayMate collects the following data:

  • Location data: Your device's GPS location is used to detect when you are near a UK airport. This is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA): Used by Google AdMob to serve advertisements. You will be asked for permission before this is accessed.
  • App usage data: Basic crash reports and performance data may be collected anonymously via Expo/React Native to help us fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Location data is used solely to detect nearby UK airports and trigger fee reminders
  • Advertising identifiers are used to display relevant ads via Google AdMob
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes

3. Location Data

AirportPayMate requests "Always On" location permission to detect airports even when the app is in the background. Your location is never stored on our servers, shared with third parties, or used for any purpose other than airport detection within the app.

4. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display ads. Google may collect and use your advertising identifier and other data to serve personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised advertising in your iPhone Settings under Privacy → Tracking. For more information, visit Google's Privacy Policy.

5. In-App Purchases

Any purchases are processed securely by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data held on your device (such as your airport history and settings) remains on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

7. Children's Privacy

AirportPayMate is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, most of these rights are exercised directly on your device. To make a request, contact us at the address below.

9. Third-Party Services

AirportPayMate uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – advertising
  • Expo – app infrastructure
  • Apple – app distribution and payments

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance.
